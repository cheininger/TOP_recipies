Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

A pasta dish with bacon, cheese and eggs for a creamy, savory sauce.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti, 500 g
  • Pancetta, 150 g
  • Onion, 1
  • Cream, 2 dL
  • Eggs, 3
  • grated Parmesan, 100 g
  • freshly ground black Pepper
  • Nutmeg, a pinch

Steps

  1. Cook Spaghetti al dente
  2. Sear the Pancetta in a pan until crispy and take out
  3. Glace the Onion in the same pan with a little fat until it is soft
  4. Add back the Spaghetti and let them get warm
  5. Mix the Cream, Eggs, Parmesan and spices and add to the pan
  6. Add back the Pancetta and mix well (avoid curdling of the Eggs)
